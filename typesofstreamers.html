Single Game Streamers

Single Game Streamers are streamers that mainly or only stream one game

In the picture: TenZ, a popular Valorant streamer & competitive player. He is currently signed to SENTINELS, an e-sports organization.

When people mention Valorant, they most likely know Tenz.

Variety Streamers

Variety streamers stream whatever they want

In the picture: Ludwig, he is an American livestreamer, YouTuber, esports commentator and competitor. He streams video-game-related content and non-video-game-related content.

He became the most subscribed Twitch streamer of all time on March 14, 2021, with 283,066 at it's peak.

IRL Streamers

IRL streamers are those who use their real face while streaming

In the picture: Disguised Toast, is a Taiwanese-Canadian streamer, YouTuber and Internet Personality. He started on YouTube then began streaming. He had an exclusive streaming contract with Facebook Gaming that ended in November 2021.

He is a member of OfflineTV, an online social entertainment group of content creators.

VTubers

Vtubers are online entertainers who use virtual avatars created using computer graphics

In the picture: Shxtou, is a male independent English-speaking Vietnamese-American VTuber. He is known for his overtly lewd and bold personality.

He has done collaborations with VTubers from NIJISANJI, a VTuber agency.
