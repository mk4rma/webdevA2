Getting Started

What is OBS Studio?

OBS Studio is a free and open software for video recoding and live streaming.

Download

1. Create a scene

After opening the software,

Click on the plus icon to create and name a scene

2. Add Sources

Likewise, Click on the plus icon to add sources to the scene. It can be audio, video and more!

3. Choose the platform of choice

Go to settings and go into the stream tab. From there, select the platform you want to stream to.

It can be Twitch, Youtube or more!

4. Ensure that everything is good

Make sure that both audio and video are detected by looking at the main interface and audio mixer.

5. Start streaming!

Click on the Start Streaming button!
